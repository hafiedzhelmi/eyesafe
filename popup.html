How to use this filter

Guide to Using the Filter

This filter helps to identify and blur sexual content in images. The sensitivity level determines the strictness of the filter. The filter will filter out the preffered sensitivity level and all levels above it.

Level Criteria
1 Explicit Nudity (men and women genitilia)
2 Full Nudity (men and women bust and bums)
3 Partial Nudity (men and women in underwear)
4 Implied Nudity (shirtless men and short dressed women)
5 Minimal Nudity (all human subject/features)
